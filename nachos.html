Nachos


Wondering what to do with your leftovers from taco night? Here's a great recipe that's easy to make. Add whatever ingredients you have leftover from taco night!

Ingredients

  • Leftover taco meat
  • Black beans or pinto beans
  • Salsa
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Diced tomato
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Tortilla chips

Instructions

  1. Place the tortilla chips on a sheet pan.
  2. Spread an even layer of reheated taco meat on the chips, followed by a layer of beans.
  3. Cover the chips, meat, and beans with a layer of shredded cheese.
  4. Bake in the oven until the cheese has melted.
  5. Remove the nachos from the oven and top with tomato and lettuce.
  6. Serve with salsa.
