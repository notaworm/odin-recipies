Tacos

Tacos are a great option for a family meal. Try these out tonight!

This taco recipe uses chicken, but feel free to substitute with a meat alternative.

Ingredients

  • Chicken or meat alternative
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Small or medium size flour tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced Tomato
  • Salsa
  • Fresh Lime
  • Taco seasoning

Directions

  1. Cook the chicken or meat alternative with the taco seasoning until fully cooked.
  2. Place the chicken on the tortillas. Do not overload the tortillas.
  3. Add the cheese, tomato, and lettuce to the tortillas.
  4. Garnish with a squeeze of fresh lime.
  5. Serve with salsa on the side.
