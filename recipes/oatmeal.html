Oatmeal


Looking for an easy breakfast to make for the whole family? Try this oatmeal recipe!

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Blackberries
  • Strawberries
  • Brown sugar or maple syrup

Directions

  1. Combine the milk and oats.
  2. Cook according to the package instructions.
  3. Wash and cut the berries.
  4. Add the brown sugar or maple syrup to the oatmeal according to your preference.
  5. Add the berries on top of the oatmeal.
  6. Eat.
